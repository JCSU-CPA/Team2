IB Learner Profiles

Open-Minded

Open-Minded scholars are ones who are open to the ideas of others, and fully respect those ideas just the same.

Reflective

Relective scholars the ones who return to what they have accomplished to see what could of been done beter and to learn from those.

Caring

Caring scholars are the ones that show kindness to others around them

Inquirer

Inquirer scholars are the ones that are curious about the world and ask questions based on their curosity.

Communicator

Communicator scholars are the ones who have the ability to speak out amongst others on ideas, and can listen to other people speaking on their ideas.

Thinker

Thinkers are the ones that solve their way through problems with critical thinking, to come up with the best solution.

Knowledgeable

Knowledgeable scholars are the ones that know many pieces of information and can remember that information.

Principled

Principled scholars are the ones that do the right thng even if there is nobody watching and tells the truth.

Risk-Taker

Risk-Takers are the scholars who aren't afraid to try new things and fail, they get back up again when they fail.

Well Balanced

Well Balanced scholars are the ones who keep a positive attitude and mindset.
