About Us


Ranson IB Middle School is a school based on building communities with one another.
Communities that brings students together when the time is needed, as a big family.
We ensure you that if you are going through something, you will not walk the path alone.
Focused on building leaders, Ranson hopes to create a better world in change from student leaders.
Here at Ranson IB, we follow certain characteristics that personify an IB scholar.
These are classified as IB learner profiles, you can view these profiles here.
Staff strengthen these profiles towards students to build world leaders that WILL make an impactful change(s) in the world.
We believe that building world leaders this young in a scholars lifetime is essential and necessary for the brightest future.
Strengthening bonds in the form of communities helps students learn to communicate with one another, strengthening communication skills as the IB learner profile.
This is important as growth in these important IB learner profiles are what make world leaders that make a change.
